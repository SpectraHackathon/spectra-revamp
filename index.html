Exporing the Tech Space Together

Spectra is a brand-new all-ladies hackathon coming to the West Coast! Join us from July 22nd to July 24th for 24 awesome hours of hacking with over 400 fellow women in technology.

This is a unique event bringing together female high schoolers, college students, and women in tech supporters from all around the world. Free from the gender stereotypes that can often taint the male-dominated tech industry, Spectra offers hackers a safe and inviting environment for women to experiment and work comfortably with technology at any experience level.

Every woman should have the opportunity to gain confidence in her technical abilities and be motivated to make her own impact in computer science. So what are you waiting for? Come learn new skills, inspire one another, and build long-lasting connections at Spectra.
